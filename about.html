Our story

Dallas's Go-To Auto Care Team

To provide honest, high-quality automotive repair and body work that keeps our community safe and mobile.

Ramirez Auto Repair, Body Shop & Wheel Alignment LLC was founded with a simple mission: deliver expert automotive care with integrity. What started as a small garage has grown into a full-service shop trusted by Dallas drivers for over 15 years. Our founder, a master technician with decades of experience, built the business on the belief that every customer deserves transparent communication, fair pricing, and work that lasts.

Today, our team of ASE-certified mechanics and body specialists serves thousands of vehicles each year. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and training to stay ahead of evolving automotive technology. Whether you need a quick oil change or a complete engine rebuild, we treat every car as if it were our own. We're proud to be a family-owned business that puts people first.
