Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care Since 2008

To provide honest, high-quality auto repairs and body work at fair prices, treating every customer like family.

Ramirez Auto Repair, Body Shop & Wheel Alignment LLC was founded by the Ramirez family with a simple mission: deliver reliable automotive service with integrity. Starting as a small shop on S Fitzhugh Ave, we've grown by earning the trust of Dallas drivers through consistent quality and transparent communication.

Today, our team of certified technicians continues that tradition. We invest in the latest equipment and training to serve modern vehicles, while keeping the personal touch that sets us apart. Whether it's a routine oil change or a complex collision repair, we treat your car as if it were our own.
