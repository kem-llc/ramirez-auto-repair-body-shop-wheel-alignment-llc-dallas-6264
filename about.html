Our story

Dallas's Trusted Family-Owned Auto Repair Shop

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair and body shop services that keep our community safe and moving. We treat every customer like family and every car like our own.

Ramirez Auto Repair was founded over 15 years ago with a simple mission: deliver expert automotive service with integrity. What started as a small two-bay shop has grown into a full-service facility serving Dallas and the surrounding areas. Our founder, a master mechanic with decades of experience, built the business on the belief that customers deserve transparent communication and fair pricing.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of ASE-certified technicians who share a passion for cars and a commitment to excellence. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and training to ensure we can handle any repair, from a simple oil change to a complex engine rebuild. Our loyal customers return because they trust us to be honest, efficient, and skilled.
