Body Shop & Collision Repair

Professional collision repair, dent removal, and custom paint jobs to restore your vehicle's appearance.

Overview

Our body shop is equipped to handle everything from minor scratches to major collision damage. We use computer-aided measuring systems for precise frame alignment and factory-matched paint for a seamless finish. We also assist with insurance claims and provide rental car coordination.

What's included

  • Restores vehicle safety and structural integrity
  • Flawless color-matched paint that lasts
  • Hassle-free insurance claim processing
  • Fast turnaround to get you back on the road

How it works

Common questions

Will my insurance cover the repair?

We work with all major insurance companies and can help you file a claim. Most collision repairs are covered under comprehensive or collision coverage.

How long does a typical body repair take?

Minor repairs can be done in a day, while major collision work may take a week or more. We'll give you a timeline upfront.
