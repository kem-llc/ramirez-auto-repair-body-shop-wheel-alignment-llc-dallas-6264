Diagnostics & Electrical Systems

Advanced computer diagnostics and electrical repair for check engine lights, battery, alternator, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

Modern vehicles rely on complex electrical systems. Our diagnostic tools can quickly identify issues with the engine, transmission, ABS, airbags, and other electronic modules. We repair or replace alternators, starters, batteries, sensors, and wiring harnesses.

What's included

  • Accurately identifies problems without guesswork
  • Prevents unnecessary part replacements
  • Restores vehicle performance and safety features
  • Includes a detailed report of findings

How it works

1

1. Scan & Retrieve Codes

We connect our diagnostic scanner to read error codes from the vehicle's computer.

2

2. Interpret & Inspect

Our technician interprets the codes and performs visual inspections to pinpoint the root cause.

3

3. Repair & Clear Codes

We perform the necessary repair and clear the codes, then test to ensure the issue is resolved.
