Wheel Alignment & Tire Services

Precision wheel alignment, tire balancing, and tire replacement to improve handling and tire life.

Overview

Proper wheel alignment is crucial for even tire wear, stable handling, and fuel efficiency. We use state-of-the-art alignment equipment to adjust camber, caster, and toe angles to manufacturer specifications. We also offer tire rotation, balancing, and new tire installation.

What's included

  • Extends tire life and improves fuel economy
  • Enhances steering response and vehicle stability
  • Prevents vibration and uneven wear
  • Includes a free alignment check with tire purchase

How it works

1

1. Tire Inspection

We check tire condition, pressure, and tread depth to determine if rotation or replacement is needed.

2

2. Alignment Setup

Your vehicle is mounted on our alignment rack, and we adjust angles to factory specs.

3

3. Test Drive & Verification

We take a test drive to confirm the alignment is correct and the steering feels straight.

Common questions

How do I know if I need an alignment?

Signs include uneven tire wear, your car pulling to one side, or a crooked steering wheel when driving straight.

How often should I get an alignment?

It's recommended every 10,000-12,000 miles or whenever you install new tires.
