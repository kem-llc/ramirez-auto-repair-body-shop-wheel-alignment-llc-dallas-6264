Diagnostic & Electrical Services

Advanced computer diagnostics and electrical system repair.

Schedule this service

Overview

Modern vehicles rely on complex electrical systems. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair issues with batteries, alternators, starters, sensors, wiring, and onboard computers. We use OEM-level scan tools to read and interpret fault codes accurately.

What's included

  • Quickly identifies the root cause of warning lights
  • Prevents electrical failures that leave you stranded
  • Restores all electronic features and performance
  • Compatible with all makes and models

How it works

1

Code Reading & Analysis

We connect a professional scan tool to read diagnostic trouble codes and live data.

2

System Testing

We test individual components like the battery, alternator, and sensors to isolate the fault.

3

Repair & Reset

We replace faulty parts and clear codes, then test the system to confirm the fix.

Common questions

What does the check engine light mean?

It can indicate anything from a loose gas cap to a serious engine problem. We recommend a diagnostic scan to determine the cause.

Can you fix electrical issues on luxury cars?

Yes, we have the tools and training to work on all makes and models, including luxury and imported vehicles.
